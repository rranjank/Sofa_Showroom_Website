How to Choose the Perfect Sofa for Your Home

When it comes to designing your living space, the sofa is the heart of your home. It is where you relax, spend time with family, and welcome guests.

1. Consider Your Space First
A small room needs a compact sofa, while a large space can have sectional or L-shaped sofas.

2. Choose the Right Style
Modern sofas suit contemporary homes, while traditional sofas give a classic look.

3. Focus on Fabric & Material
Fabric sofas are comfortable, while leather sofas give a premium feel.

4. Comfort is Key
Always check cushion softness and back support.

5. Functionality
Choose sofa cum beds or storage sofas for smart use of space.

6. Budget Matters
Invest in quality sofas that last long.
